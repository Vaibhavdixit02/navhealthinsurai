| # | Hypothesis | Source | Status |
|---|------------|--------|--------|
| 1 | **Self-insured employers <500 employees.** Directly incentivized to optimize navigation (full exposure to unnecessary expense). Monthly feedback cycles vs. annual for fully insured. Smaller populations have more acute pain in predicting total claims. | V1 Memo, p.8–9 | Proposed in memo. | 3 | **Captive roll-up.** Acquire small independent captive health plan operators (30–50 exist, long tail). Solves cold-start membership + data. Layer AI on acquired books. Pitch to employers: "Same captive structure.
