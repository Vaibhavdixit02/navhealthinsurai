# ICP

**Last updated:** 2026-04-02

## Primary

**Self-insured employers ≤500 employees.** ~325K potential customers (300K small + 25K mid-size), employing ~10–12M workers. Directly incentivized to optimize navigation, monthly feedback cycles, acute pain in predicting total claims for smaller populations.
*(V1 Memo, p.8–10)*

## Secondary

**DTC consumers.** No onboarding required. Builds user base, aggregates front-door data, informs B2B targeting. Multiple activation paths: employer auto-enrollment, DTC with plan data upload, DTC without plan data.
*(V1 Memo, p.5–6)*

**Small captive health plan operators (acquisition targets).** Founder-led firms running 10–40 employer groups each, many approaching succession with no clear buyer. Typical target: ~6K covered lives, ~$45M managed premium, valued at $3–4M.
*(Email: "Open questions", 2026-03-09, Vaibhav)*

## Not targeting

- **India** — deprioritized. *(Email: "Re: AI-Native Health Plan", 2026-03-09, Manav)*
- **Large employers / payors / providers** — lengthier sales cycles, Phase 2+. *(V1 Memo, p.9)*
- **Fully insured employers** — annual feedback cycles, second-order effects only. *(V1 Memo, p.8–9)*
