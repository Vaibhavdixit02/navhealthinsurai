## India

Not building for India currently. "The market dynamics are so different given the healthcare system is dramatically different from the US." Also requires being physically in India. *(Email: "Re: AI-Native Health Plan", 2026-03-09, Manav)*

India market dominated by banks' insurance products, very low participation from standalone companies. Biggest blockers are licensing and permissions. Hinduja family owns a health insurance license and is actively looking to offload it. *(WhatsApp, 2026-03-07–08, Vaibhav + Manav)*
